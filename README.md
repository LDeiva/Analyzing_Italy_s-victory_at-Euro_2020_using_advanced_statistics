## Group Stage, How Italy Played? 

Italy dominated its group and it allows us to understand the tactical principles with which Coach Mancini sets up the team. 

The number about the offensive productions generated by Italy and that allowed to the other teams help us to describe this domain.

<img width="1472" height="642" alt="Team Shots Group Stage" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/b021d2d5-90ae-4a2d-8d56-e3102c1367a6" />

During group Stage Italy every 90 minutes was the fifth team for XG produced (1.60 XG P90) after Netherlands, Spain, Portugal and Denmark but the third for Goal scored (1.94 Goal P90) under only Netherlands and Portugal (Own goals were not counted in this analyses). 

Exluding penalty (Italy had no penalty in favor during group stage) Italy every 90 minute was the fourth team for npXG every (1.6 npXG P90) and second for npGoal scored (1.94 npGoal P90). 

But it gave the best in defense, positioning like the best defense of the group stage (0.23 XG and npXG P90) and with 0 Goal conceded.

In addiction of the quality of actions Italy generated a big volume of shots positioning like the best Team for Shots every 90 Minute (19.42 Shots P90) and for non penalty Shots on target (6.15 npShots on target P90).
